Spicy One Skillet Lasagna

What is a Dinner Spicy One Skillet Lasagna?

Made and orginated from Giada De Laurentiis,this dish is made of pasta, cream cheese and Italian famous sausage!

Recipe:

Total: 1 hr 25 min

Prep: 10 min

Cook: 1 hr 15 min

Inactive time: 40 min

level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 pounds spicy Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 3 shallots, finely minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest (from 2 lemons)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 bunch broccoli rabe, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • One 25-ounce jar marinara sauce
  • 8 ounces no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella, dried well and torn into large chunks
  • 1/2 cup grated Gruyere

Instuctions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  2. In a 12-inch high-sided skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat
  3. Add the sausage and cook, breaking it into bite-size pieces and cook for 7 minutes
  4. Add the shallots and garlic and cook for another 3 minutes
  5. Add the lemon zest and red pepper flakes and cook for additional 2 minutes to toast
  6. Stir in the broccoli rabe and salt, cook for 2 minutes
  7. Remove the mixture to a medium bowl and return the pan to the stove over low heat
  8. Spoon a small amount of the marinara sauce in the bottom of the skillet
  9. Mix the remaining marinara with the sausage mixture in the bowl
  10. Layer half of the lasagna noodles on top of the marinara, breaking them up as needed to fit the pan
  11. Cover with half of the sausage mixture.
  12. Add 3/4 cup of the Parmesan and half the mozzarella
  13. Layer with the remaining noodles, covering the remaining sausage mixture
  14. Top with the rest of the Parmesan and mozzarella and the Gruyere.
  15. Bake until bubbly and golden on top, about 40 minutes. Cool for 5 minutes before serving.
