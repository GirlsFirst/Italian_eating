Who is Giada De Laurentiis?

Giada Pamela De Laurentiis is an Italian-born American chef, writer, and television personality. She is the host of Food Network's Giada at Home. She also appears regularly as a contributor and guest co-host on NBC's Today. For more information on Giada De Laurentiis, visit her information site on Food Network.
