Chicken Salad Sandwhich

What Is A Chicken Salad Sandwich?

It Is Perfectly Seasoned And Tender Chicken Mixed With Fresh, Crunchy Textures Of Apple, Celery, Pomegranate Seeds And Walnuts.

Ingredients For Chicken

  • 1 boneless and skinless chicken breast
  • 1l water, plus extra for steaming
  • 20g of salt
  • 10g of sugar

Ingredients For Salad

  • 1/2 apple, small green variety, diced
  • 1 celery stick, diced
  • 1/2 orange, zested
  • 1 tbsp of mustard
  • 3 tbsp of mayonnaise
Extra Ingredients
  • 2 ciabatta buns
  • 2 tsp pomegranate seeds
  • 2 tsp chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. Combine The Water, Salt, And Sugar Together In A Large Bowl.
  2. Stir The Mixture Well
  3. Place The Chicken Into The Bowl. Brine For 1 Hour.
  4. Drain The Chicken From The Brine.
  5. Place Chicken Onto A Deep Baking Tray. Pour 1cm Of Water.
  6. Cover Tray With Foil. Place The Tray Into The Oven And Steam The Chicken For 20 Minutes Or Until It's Tender And Cooked Through.
  7. Once Chicken Is Finished, Dice The Chicken Into Small Cubes And Place Them Into A Bowl With Diced Apples, Celery, And Orange Zest.
  8. In A Seperate Bowl, Whisk The Mustard And Mayo Together To Form A Creamy Dressing.
  9. Place Mixture Into The Chicken Salad Bowl And Stir Everything. Make Sure Everything Is Evenly Coated
  10. Halve The Buns And Fill With The Chicken Mixture. Add Pomegranate Seeds And Walnut Pieces To The Taste.
  11. Now Your Meal Is Complete. "MANGIA"
